What We Offer

OSINT Lab offers users a large quantity challenges ranging from difficulty levels of beginner, easy, medium, and hard, that cover 4 of the major OSINT topics:

  • SOCMINT - Analysis of data from social media platforms to gather information and insights.
  • GEOINT - Gathering and analyzing data related to geographical locations and features.
  • SIGINT - Interception and analysis of electronic signals and communications.
  • MISC - Various other sources and methods of intelligence gathering not covered by the main categories.

User Data

We ensure that all user data is securely stored and all account passwords are properly hashed. We only ask for your country in order to plan our future outreach and no data will ever be sold or exchanged with any third parties.

Future Plans

Simply practicing OSINT isn't enough to help everyone get better. In the future, we're planning on adding a learning page that offers beginners tutorials/guides for starting on OSINT as well as more advanced lessons for those more experienced who are looking to hone their skills even further. Additionally, we will also add a profile page that will allow users to customize their display and help connect with others, improving each user's overall OSINTing experience
